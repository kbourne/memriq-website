// OPERATING MODEL

How an autonomous organization runs itself.

AGENTS EARN AUTONOMY

Trust is earned, not granted.

New agents require approval for every action. After 10 successful decisions, routine actions auto-approve. After 50, most actions do. Authority compounds with track record.

MESSAGEBUS IS THE NERVOUS SYSTEM

Every interaction is a learning signal.

All agent-to-agent communication runs asynchronous pub/sub. Every message is observed, scored, and folded back into procedural memory. The organization improves with every cycle.

GOALS, MEMORY, BELIEFS

Agents that know what they know.

Every agent has measurable objectives, three types of memory — semantic, episodic, procedural — and Bayesian confidence in its own outputs. Agents hedge when uncertain and escalate when stuck.
